What is Swiggy One?

Swiggy One is a membership program that offers unlimited free delivery on thousands of restaurants and other benefits.

I want an invoice for my order

You can find the invoice in the "My Orders" section under the specific order details.

How can I edit my order?

Orders cannot be edited once placed. However, you can cancel within a few seconds and place a new one.

My payment was debited but order not placed

Don't worry, the amount will be automatically refunded to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.
